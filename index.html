G Suite Modifiers

What are G Suite Modifiers?

The tools here change G Suite share and publishing links (Docs, Sheets, Slides) to achieve a variety of actions that are useful, but are not common knowledge.

What can these modifiers do that makes them unique?

These modifiers can cut down on time changing the share and publishing links through only having to paste in the links to the spaces provided and generate your new link or code for the required action.

Why use these modifiers?

These modifiers can help support collaboration with students and also display information dynamically through embedding G Suite documents and modifiying share links.

How do I use these modifiers?

On each of the pages there are step by step instructions on what input you need to provide, and how to manipulate the respective element to produce the final result desired. Each page will also produce a preview. This is so you can take a sneak peek at what you have created before pasting it into Moodle and make and changes you feel are necessary and regenerate code until you reach the desired result.
